TAMETEQ · A CONCEPT FOR HEDEPY

Trust the data before an automation acts on it.

This short concept follows Hedepy’s public AI & Automation Manager opening: recurring marketing work across nine markets, connected ad platforms, CRM and analytics—with human approval where spend is affected.

Proposed control layer
1. ObserveCollect the agreed metrics from ad platforms, CRM and analytics.
2. ValidateDetect missing mappings, outliers and definition mismatches before reporting.
3. ExplainGive the owner a short diagnostic with its evidence, not a black-box score.
4. Approve and actKeep a human in the loop for spend-impacting changes and record the decision.

Safe pilot

Start with one market, a small set of agreed metrics and a single human-approved action.

Measure: data-quality issues caught before action, time saved and the confidence needed to extend the model.

No Hedepy data has been accessed or processed for this concept.
